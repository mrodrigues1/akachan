• TimerDisplay rewritten: Canvas-based conic arc (progress = elapsed / maxMinutes). Ring colour = primary (pink for feed, blue for sleep).
• Ring fill stops at 100% and ring turns amber if session exceeds max time setting.
• SideSelector replaced with two big square buttons (same big-touch pattern as your choice).

• Sleep type selector uses same big-button pattern for visual consistency with side selector.
• "History" link moved to TopAppBar trailing icon — frees vertical space on small devices.
